# üìä Atlas Labs HR Analytics with Power BI

## üìù Executive Summary
This project delivers a comprehensive HR analytics solution for Atlas Labs, built entirely in Power BI. It focuses on monitoring workforce health, analyzing performance and satisfaction trends, and identifying key drivers of employee attrition. The report is powered by a robust data model, dynamic dashboards, and validated DAX measures that enable stakeholders to explore insights interactively and make data-driven decisions.

## üíº Business Problem
Atlas Labs needed a centralized, visual solution to understand workforce dynamics and improve employee retention. The challenge was to uncover patterns in attrition, performance, and demographics, and to provide actionable insights that HR and leadership could use to enhance employee experience and reduce turnover.

## üîß Methodology
- Data ingestion and transformation using Power Query  
- Snowflake data model design with a custom DimDate table  
- Creation of active/inactive relationships for time-aware analysis  
- Development of modular, reusable DAX measures for KPIs  
- Design of four interactive dashboards: Overview, Demographics, Performance Tracker, and Attrition  
- Validation of all metrics against source data for accuracy and consistency

## üõ†Ô∏è Skills and Tools Used
- **Power BI** ‚Äì Data modeling, dashboard design, DAX  
- **Power Query** ‚Äì Data transformation and cleaning  
- **DAX** ‚Äì KPI creation, time intelligence, business logic  
- **Microsoft Word** ‚Äì Documentation and reporting  
- **Data Visualization** ‚Äì Cards, charts, slicers, filters  
- **Analytical Thinking** ‚Äì Trend analysis, cohort evaluation, performance tracking


## üìà Results
- Built four dynamic dashboards tailored to HR needs  
- Enabled filtering by employee, department, and time period  
- Identified high-risk attrition segments and satisfaction-performance correlations  
- Delivered clear, validated KPIs for headcount, attrition, satisfaction, and performance  
- Provided a scalable framework for ongoing workforce analysis

## üìå Business Recommendations
- Review and adjust policies for departments with high attrition  
- Monitor overtime and travel frequency as potential stress factors  
- Invest in manager development and onboarding improvements  
- Use satisfaction and performance trends to guide employee engagement strategies  
- Continue tracking KPIs to measure the impact of interventions over time
